JoSAA 2025-26 Fee Structure and Required Documents
Required Documents for JoSAA 2025 Counselling
1. Class 10 Certificate - For date of birth verification.
2. Class 12 Mark Sheet and Certificate - Proof of qualifying examination.
3. JEE Main/Advanced Admit Card - Depending on the institute type.
4. Category Certificate (if applicable) - SC/ST/OBC-NCL/GEN-EWS.
5. PwD Certificate (if applicable) - For Persons with Disabilities.
6. Medical Certificate - As per the prescribed format.
7. Passport/Citizenship Certificate (if applicable) - For foreign nationals.
8. OCI/PIO Card (if applicable) - For Overseas Citizens of India/Persons of Indian Origin.
9. Bank Account Details - For refund purposes.
JoSAA 2025 Fee Structure
1. Seat Acceptance Fee (SAF)
This fee confirms your seat after allotment and includes a non-refundable processing charge of Rs.
5,000.
Category Total SAF Processing Fee Refundable
General/OBC-NCL/GEN-EWS Rs.35,000 Rs.5,000 Rs.30,000
SC/ST/PwD Rs.17,500 Rs.5,000 Rs.12,500
2. Partial Admission Fee (PAF)
Applicable only to candidates allotted seats in the NIT+ System (NITs, IIITs, GFTIs) after the final
round.
Category PAF Amount
General/OBC-NCL/GEN-EWS Rs.45,000
SC/ST/PwD Rs.20,000
Payment Methods
- Net Banking
- Debit/Credit Card
- UPI
- e-Challan (State Bank of India)
Refund Policy
- Withdrawal from the Process - Refund available after deducting Rs.7,000 processing charges.
- Seat Cancellation due to Eligibility/Document Issues - Refund available after deducting Rs.5,000
processing charges.
- Once paid, the seat acceptance fee is non-refundable unless specified above.
